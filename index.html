Why you should you hire me

Hello World! My name is Matt Jones and I am a passionate web developer and designer.
I love coding, problem-solving and building unique projects from the wild ideas that keep me up at night!

Learning is never ending; new coding skills, exploring new territories... I am constantly developing and building creative things. I have studied Python for a number of years which taught me how to think like a programmer and gave me an excellent foundation upon which to understand both front and back-end development.

Prior to being a developer, I was the founder & CEO of a music technology company working with schools, youth centres and prisons in the UK for 10 years. I would say my background is fairly unique in that I have always had to break the mold to carve my own path to success.
